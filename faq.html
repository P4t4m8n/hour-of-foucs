Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main goal of My Nonprofit?

Our primary goal is to empower local communities through education, resource distribution, and advocacy efforts.

How can I volunteer?

You can sign up via our Contact Page or email us directly at volunteer@my-nonprofit.org.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes. We are a registered 501(c)(3) organization, so all donations are tax-deductible.

Where do donations go?

Donations primarily fund community outreach programs, workshops, operational costs, and educational materials.
